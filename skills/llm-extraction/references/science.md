# Science Profile

Use this profile for synthesis-level plus monitoring-summary extraction, especially when a benchmark paper aggregates many studies into region, period, and pollutant-group summaries.

## Extraction level

- always capture study metadata
- capture structured concentration or trend summaries when clearly reported
- region × period × pollutant-group extraction is preferred when that is the benchmark row shape
- full record splitting is optional only when tables make it reliable and when the benchmark truly needs it

## Focus

- pollutant or pollutant-group identity
- matrix and environmental compartment
- geographic context
- sampling period or temporal window
- key summary statistics, trend direction, or ecological risk signals
- figure/table provenance and geo-locatability
