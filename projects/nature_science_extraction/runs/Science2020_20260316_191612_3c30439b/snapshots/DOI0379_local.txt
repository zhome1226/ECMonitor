Data on dental caries is presented as the DMFT index (dmft for deciduous teeth) and records the tooth specific cumulative effect of dental caries experience: decayed (D), missing (M) or filled (F) because of dental caries for per- manent teeth (T). The surface specific (DMFS/dmfs) index records the caries experience of each of the tooth surfaces. In children the prevalence of gingivitis was reported as a modified gingival index.11 The Community Periodontal Index (CPI)12 was used to measure periodontal status for the adult survey.
